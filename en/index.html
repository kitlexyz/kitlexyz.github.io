Ksteps is an app designed to help you check your daily step count and goal progress at a glance on Apple Watch. This page provides both support information and the privacy policy for Ksteps.

About Ksteps

Ksteps is designed to help you quickly check your step count on Apple Watch and complications. You can set your step goal, language, and unit preferences, and the app reads walking data from Apple Health to show your progress.

Things to Know

  • When you launch the app on Apple Watch for the first time, you may see a permission request for Apple HealthKit.
  • It may take a few seconds for the system permission sheet to respond.
  • Your step count can refresh when you open the Apple Watch app or manually refresh it.

Data We Use

Ksteps reads walking data from Apple HealthKit, such as your step count, so it can display that information in the app and complications.

How We Use Data

  • To display your step count and goal progress in the app and on Apple Watch complications.
  • To save your preferences such as step goal, language, unit, and complication title on your device and in shared app storage.

Server Transmission

Ksteps does not send your HealthKit data to a developer server and does not store it separately outside your device.

Third-Party Sharing

Ksteps does not share your HealthKit data with third parties.
