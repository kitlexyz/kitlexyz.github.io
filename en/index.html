Support & Privacy

An app designed to let you quickly check today's step count and goal progress on Apple Watch. This page provides both Ksteps support information and the privacy policy.

Support

About the App

Ksteps is designed to quickly show your step count on Apple Watch and its complications. You can configure your step goal, language, and units, and the app reads walking data from Apple Health to display your progress.

Things to Check When Using the App

  • When you run the app on Apple Watch for the first time, a HealthKit permission request may appear.
  • It may take a few seconds for the permission sheet to appear.
  • Your step count may update when you open the Apple Watch app or refresh it.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Ksteps may read walking data from Apple HealthKit (such as step count) to display it within the app and complications.

How Information Is Used

  • Used to display step count and goal progress in the app and Apple Watch complications.
  • Settings such as step goals, language, units, and complication titles may be stored on the device or in the app group storage.

Server Transmission

Ksteps does not transmit or store HealthKit data on the developer's servers.

Third-Party Sharing

Ksteps does not share HealthKit data with third parties.
